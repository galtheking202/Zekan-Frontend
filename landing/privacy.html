Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Welcome to ZeKan ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "Application") available on the Google Play Store and Apple App Store.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By downloading and using the Application, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

To provide you with hyper-local news and real-time notifications without unnecessary "background noise," we collect certain information:

A. Location Data (Critical for App Functionality)

Precise Location: Because ZeKan delivers news based on your exact vicinity, the Application requires access to your device's precise location services (GPS and network-based).

Background Location (Optional but recommended for alerts): If you grant permission, the Application may access your location even when the app is closed or not in use, solely to send you critical, real-time push notifications regarding urgent local events or road closures directly relevant to your immediate physical area.

B. Device and Usage Data

We may collect standard device information, such as your mobile device ID, operating system version, device type, and basic usage statistics (e.g., how you interact with articles) to improve app performance and stability.

C. Personal Data

No Mandatory Account Registration: ZeKan allows instant access without requiring a personal account, name, or password.

User-Generated Content: If you choose to report an event or submit a local update, we collect the text, image, and location associated with that specific submission.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To filter and display relevant local news based on your location.
  • To send targeted, urgent push notifications about critical events nearby.
  • To maintain, optimize, and improve the performance of the Application.
  • To prevent fraud, monitor spam, and ensure the safety of our community guidelines.

3. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

We value your privacy and do not sell your data. We only share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Cloud Infrastructure: We host our services using secure cloud providers (such as Railway) and utilize infrastructure services (such as Firebase Cloud Messaging) solely to process data and deliver push notifications.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

4. Apple iOS and Google Android Specific Disclosures

For iOS Users (Apple App Store)

  • App Tracking Transparency (ATT): We do not track your activity across third-party apps or websites for advertising purposes.
  • Location Permissions: You can manage or revoke location permissions at any time via your iOS Settings → Privacy → Location Services → ZeKan.

For Android Users (Google Play Store)

  • Google Play Developer Policies: We comply strictly with Google's Prominent Disclosure requirements regarding location data. Background location is used strictly to trigger urgent local push notifications.
  • You can modify or turn off location access at any time through your Android Device Settings → Apps → ZeKan → Permissions.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Location History: We do not keep a permanent history of your movements. Your current location token is updated dynamically to serve you local news and is overwritten regularly.
  • User Contributions: Content submitted by users (such as public local reports) will remain on our servers as part of the local news feed until deleted by us or requested by the creator.

6. Children's Privacy

Our services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
